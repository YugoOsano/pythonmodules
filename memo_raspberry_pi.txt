color   1st,2nd multiplier error

black   0       1
brown   1       10
red     2       10^2
orange  3       10^3
yellow  4       10^4
green   5       10^5
blue    6       10^6
violet  7       10^7
gray    8
white   9       0.001
gold            0.1        +-5%
silver          0.01       +-10%
